# Pokemon Habitat Background Images

This folder contains background images for different Pokemon habitats.

## Required Images

Please add the following images (recommended size: 800x600px or similar aspect ratio):

1. `cave.jpg` or `cave.png` - Cave/underground habitat
2. `forest.jpg` or `forest.png` - Forest/woods habitat
3. `grassland.jpg` or `grassland.png` - Grassland/plains habitat
4. `mountain.jpg` or `mountain.png` - Mountain/rocky habitat
5. `rare.jpg` or `rare.png` - Rare/special locations habitat
6. `rough-terrain.jpg` or `rough-terrain.png` - Rough terrain habitat
7. `sea.jpg` or `sea.png` - Ocean/deep water habitat
8. `urban.jpg` or `urban.png` - City/urban habitat
9. `waters-edge.jpg` or `waters-edge.png` - Shore/beach habitat

## Image Guidelines

- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Dimensions**: 800x600px recommended (or 4:3 aspect ratio)
- **Style**: Use subtle, low-contrast images that won't interfere with Pokemon sprites and text
- **Optimization**: Keep file sizes reasonable (<200KB per image)
- **Licensing**: Ensure you have rights to use the images

## Finding Images

You can find suitable images from:
- Free stock photo sites (Unsplash, Pexels, Pixabay)
- Pokemon game screenshots (ensure proper licensing)
- AI-generated images
- Custom illustrations

## Usage

The app will automatically load these images based on each Pokemon's habitat from the PokeAPI species endpoint.
